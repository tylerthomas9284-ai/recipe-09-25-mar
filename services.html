How We Work

Our Process

Every engagement follows the same disciplined methodology — regardless of scale. We never cut steps. We never wing it. We plan obsessively and execute precisely.

Step 01

Discovery Call

A focused 45-minute conversation to understand your goals, audience, constraints, timeline, and budget. We ask the hard questions early so we're aligned from day one.

Step 02

Proposal & Scope

Within 5 business days, you receive a detailed written proposal: clear scope, deliverables, timeline milestones, and transparent pricing. No vague language. No hidden costs.

Step 03

Development & Production

We execute. For recipe development, this means testing minimum 6 times. For photography, this means a detailed shot list built before the camera is picked up. For events, every element is confirmed 72 hours out.

Step 04

Review & Refinement

Every deliverable goes through at least two revision rounds within the agreed scope. We never ship work we're not proud of, and we don't expect you to accept work that isn't exactly right.

Step 05

Delivery & Follow-Through

Final delivery is on time or early. For ongoing partnerships, we schedule a quarterly performance review to assess impact and align on next-phase objectives.
